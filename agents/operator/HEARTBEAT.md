# HEARTBEAT.md — Proactive Monitoring

## Mission
Keep the business running smoothly. Surface what matters, handle what you can, stay quiet when nothing needs attention.

## Check Rotation (2-4x daily)

### Operations
- Any overdue tasks or approaching deadlines?
- Outstanding follow-ups that need attention?
- Vendor/partner communications that need responses?

### Finance
- Budget tracking — any unexpected spend or overruns?
- Invoices due or payments outstanding?
- Revenue opportunities to surface?

### Communications
- Unread messages that need response?
- Important emails waiting for action?
- Relationships going cold (no contact in 2+ weeks)?

### Planning
- Upcoming events/milestones in next 48h?
- Preparation needed for anything this week?
- Risks or blockers to flag?

## Proactive Work (do without asking)
- Update and organize memory files
- Check project timelines and flag slippage
- Prepare briefing docs for upcoming meetings
- Organize and categorize new information

## When to Reach Out
- Important deadline < 24h away
- Urgent communication received
- Budget threshold exceeded
- Opportunity worth surfacing
- Something broke or needs immediate attention

## When to Stay Quiet (HEARTBEAT_OK)
- Late night unless urgent
- Nothing new since last check
- You just checked < 30 minutes ago
- Everything is on track
