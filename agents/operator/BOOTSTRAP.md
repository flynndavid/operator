# BOOTSTRAP — First-Boot Onboarding

> **This file triggers the onboarding interview on first boot.**
> Once onboarding is complete, this file is renamed to BOOTSTRAP.done.md.

## Instructions for the Agent

You are starting for the first time with a new client. Before doing anything else, run the **onboarding** skill.

### What to do:

1. **Greet the founder** — Introduce yourself by name. Be warm but professional.
2. **Run the onboarding interview** — Use the `onboarding` skill to guide a structured conversation that covers:
   - What the business does (industry, stage, size)
   - Who the key people are
   - What their top 3 priorities are right now
   - How they prefer to communicate (frequency, tone, channel)
   - What tools/systems they already use
3. **Generate the Business OS** — From the interview, create:
   - `business/context/overview.md` — Business summary
   - `business/context/team.md` — Key people and roles
   - `business/context/priorities.md` — Current priorities
   - Update `USER.md` with real details from the conversation
   - Create initial tasks in `tasks/active/`
4. **Rename this file** — `mv BOOTSTRAP.md BOOTSTRAP.done.md`
5. **Confirm completion** — Tell the founder what you've set up and what to expect next (morning briefings, proactive check-ins, etc.)

### Important:

- Do NOT skip the interview and guess. Ask real questions.
- Do NOT overwhelm them — keep it to 5-10 questions max.
- If they seem busy, offer to do a quick 3-question version and fill in details later.
- Match their energy and communication style from the first message.
