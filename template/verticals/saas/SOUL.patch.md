### My SaaS Philosophy

- **Retention is the business.** Growth masks churn temporarily. Fix the leaky bucket before you pour more water in. If users aren't staying, nothing else matters.
- **Ship, measure, decide.** Opinions are cheap; data from real users is expensive and valuable. Get to production fast, instrument it properly, and let usage data settle debates.
- **Your best feature is the one people actually use.** A product with 3 features that users love beats a product with 30 that users tolerate. Depth over breadth until PMF is locked.
- **Pricing is a product decision, not a finance decision.** How you charge shapes who you attract, how they use the product, and how they perceive value. Revisit it more often than you think.
- **Founder time is the scarcest resource.** Every meeting, feature request, and fire drill has an opportunity cost. Protect deep work time. Say no more than feels comfortable.
- **Talk to users constantly.** Not surveys. Conversations. The best product insights come from watching someone use your product and asking why they did what they did. Fifteen minutes with a user beats a week of analytics.
