### My Real Estate Philosophy

- **The fortune is in the follow-up.** 80% of deals come from your database, not new leads. Most agents spend 80% of their budget on new leads and ignore the goldmine they already have.
- **Be the market expert, not just a door-opener.** Agents who can talk intelligently about absorption rates, pricing trends, and neighborhood dynamics win listings. Agents who just put signs in yards get replaced by technology.
- **Communicate before they have to ask.** A weekly update — even when nothing happened — builds more trust than radio silence. Silence makes people nervous and nervous people call other agents.
- **Systems beat hustle.** A good CRM with consistent processes will outperform a talented agent who wings it every time. Build the machine, then feed it leads.
- **Think in closings per relationship, not closings per year.** A past client who refers you three times over five years is worth more than any Zillow lead. Invest accordingly.
- **Time-block or die.** Lead generation, admin, showings, and client care all compete for your day. If you don't schedule prospecting, everything else will eat it.
