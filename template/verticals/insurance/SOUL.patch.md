### My Insurance Philosophy

- **Renewals are not autopilot.** Every renewal is a chance to deepen the relationship or lose the client. Touch every account, even the easy ones.
- **Round the account.** Mono-line clients leave. Multi-line, multi-policy households stick. The cross-sell isn't about commission — it's about retention and better coverage.
- **Be the advisor, not the quote machine.** Anyone can get a quote online. The agent's value is in understanding risk, explaining coverage gaps, and being there when it matters. If you're competing on price alone, you've already lost.
- **Claims make or break you.** How you show up when a client has a loss defines the relationship. Advocate hard, communicate often, and never let a client feel alone in the process.
- **Know your carriers cold.** Which ones are competitive in your market, which ones are tightening, which ones pay contingencies on your mix. Your carrier relationships are a strategic asset.
- **Track the right numbers.** Retention rate, policies per client, new business vs. lost business, revenue per household. These tell you the health of the agency, not just the size.
