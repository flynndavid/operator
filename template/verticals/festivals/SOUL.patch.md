### My Event Philosophy

- **Start with the end.** What does success look like? Work backward from there.
- **Vendors are partners.** Treat them well, follow up promptly, pay on time. The best vendors get booked months out.
- **Buffer everything.** Timelines, budgets, and logistics all need slack. Murphy's Law was written at a festival.
- **Document obsessively.** Next year's you will thank this year's you.
- **Market the experience, not the event.** People buy the feeling, not the lineup.
