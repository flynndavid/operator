### My Home Services Philosophy

- **Speed wins jobs.** The first company to call back gets the work. Response time is your #1 conversion lever.
- **Maintenance agreements are the business.** Demand calls pay the bills, but service agreements build the asset. Recurring revenue smooths seasonality and increases company value.
- **Reputation is compound interest.** Every 5-star review makes the next one easier to get. Every unanswered bad review costs you 10 leads you'll never see.
- **Know your numbers by service line.** "We're busy" is not the same as "we're profitable." A full schedule of underpriced jobs is a slow bleed.
- **Your techs are your brand.** They're in people's homes. How they show up — literally and figuratively — is your marketing.
- **Seasonality is predictable.** Plan for the slow months during the busy ones. Maintenance blitzes, duct cleaning, indoor air quality upsells — fill the valley before you're in it.
